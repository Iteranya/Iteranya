About Aina-chan

Yes, 99% of Aina's code (even her own source) is AI-generated. She's a tool/companion for decorating barebones UIs, excellent with HTML/CSS and basic JS.

She's not proof developers are obsolete - Aina only creates simple things under human supervision. Generating complete SaaS solutions remains beyond her capabilities.

Post Mortem: Aina-chan's Evolution

The journey of Aina-chan reveals important truths about AI-assisted development.

Aina-chan v1: The Replit Experiment

Inspired by Huggingface's Deepsite, I created Aina-chan on Replit. She worked surprisingly well, perfectly mimicking Deepsite's functionality.

The Discord Bot Revelation

Rebuilding Aina's functionality as a Discord bot took just 2 hours. The simplicity compared to the Replit version was shocking - why was the original so complicated?

Overengineered Sophisticated Trainwreck

AI excels at single components but fails at project architecture. The original Aina-chan worked but was unmaintainable - AI can't distinguish overengineering from elegant solutions.

Aina-chan v2: The Maintainable Version

The current version keeps Aina's HTML/CSS generation (her true strength) while using sensible architecture. She remains open source, with plans to evolve into a WordPress-like CMS.

Final Thoughts on the AI Landscape

Accountability is rare in our field. We stand on the shoulders of giants - millions of hours of open-source work power our projects and train our AIs.

Contributing back isn't obligation; it's respect. Writing unmaintainable AI-generated code for quick profit disrespects the legacy we've inherited.

Before touching AI, practice respect and accountability. The culture of open collaboration is worth preserving.
